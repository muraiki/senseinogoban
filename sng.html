Sensei no Goban 先生の碁盤

Sensei no Goban shows all of your movements; you don't have to drop the stone. It does not force you to use the rules of Go, so it is ideal for teaching games.

Click on a stone bowl to draw a new stone. Drag and drop the stone onto the board. Others can see as you move it around.

To chat, type your name and message on the left, then press enter.
